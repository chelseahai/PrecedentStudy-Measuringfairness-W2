Design Principles

The explorable uses minimalist visuals with clear interactivity to guide understanding. Colors are used strategically to communicate group divisions, outcome probabilities, and error types.

Influences and Precedents

  • Explorable Explanation genre: Pioneered by Bret Victor and Nicky Case
  • Distill.pub: Emphasis on interactive machine learning pedagogy
  • The Pudding: Narrative visualization and scrollytelling
